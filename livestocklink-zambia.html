About Us

Solving a real problem for Zambian farmers

Zambia's livestock sector supports millions of households — yet most farmers still manage animal health through memory and paper records. The result is missed vaccinations, delayed treatment, and preventable losses that cost farmers dearly every year.

LivestockLink Zambia was built to change that. We created a simple, affordable digital platform that puts farm management tools in the hands of every farmer — from smallholders in rural districts to commercial ranches across all nine provinces.

Our goal is not just to digitize record-keeping. It is to give Zambia's livestock sector the data infrastructure it needs to grow, respond to disease, and compete at a higher level.

The Problem

Livestock losses from missed vaccinations and poor records cost Zambian farmers millions every year.

Our Vision

A Zambia where every farmer has digital tools to protect and grow their livestock business.

Our Mission

To digitize livestock management for Zambian farmers through simple, affordable, locally-built technology.

Our Impact

10,000 farmers. 9 provinces. Stronger food security for Zambia by 2028.
